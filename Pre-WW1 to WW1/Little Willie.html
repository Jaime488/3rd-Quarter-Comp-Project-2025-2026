Feature Specification
Origin United Kingdom
Manufacturer William Foster & Co. of Lincoln (Fosters of Lincoln)
Produced 1 prototype built (August–September 1915)
Mass ~16.5 tonnes (16.2 long tons)
Dimensions Length: 5.87 m (19 ft 3 in; 8.08 m including rear wheels); Width: 2.87 m; Height: 2.51 m (hull) / 3.10 m (turret)
Crew 5–6 personnel (most sources say 5)
Armor ~10 mm steel plate
Main armament Vickers 2‑pounder (40 mm) projected – never installed
Secondary armament Proposed Maxim, Hotchkiss, Lewis or Madsen MGs – none actually fitted
Engine Foster‑Daimler Knight sleeve-valve petrol engine, 105 hp
Power/weight ratio ~6 hp/tonne
Transmission Two-speed forward, one reverse with final drive via Renolds chains/ cone clutch
Suspension Unsprung (no suspension)
Operational range Unknown / No info gathered
Maximum speed 2 mph (3.2 km/h) on hard ground; ~3.5 mph (~5.6 km/h) in some reports
